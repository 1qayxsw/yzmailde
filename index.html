By authenticating, accessing, or otherwise utilizing the YZ-Systems Access Gateway ("The Infrastructure"), you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these binding corporate directives. Access is granted strictly on a least-privilege basis.

Corporate Privacy & Data Handling Policy

Compliance: SOC2 / ISO-27001

1. Data Collection Scope

In accordance with internal security mandates, YZ-Systems collects specific diagnostic and authentication telemetry during your session. This includes, but is not limited to: Originating IP address, MAC address routing tables, hardware token identifiers (FID02 serials), and biometric authentication logs provided by your Identity Provider (IdP).

2. Data Retention and Immutable Logging

All authentication events, successful or denied, are written to immutable ledger storage within the AMS Cluster. Logs are retained for a minimum of 36 months to comply with federal auditing requirements and forensic analysis protocols.

3. Employee Privacy Expectations

This is a managed corporate environment. Users should have no expectation of personal privacy regarding communications or data transmitted through the YZ-Systems Access Gateway. Personal use of this infrastructure is a violation of the Acceptable Use Policy.
